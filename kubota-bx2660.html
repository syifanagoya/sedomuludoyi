Kubota BX2660 Compact Utility Tractor is equipped with a Kubota D1005 diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled 61.1 ci [1.0 L] engine, which delivers 25.5 hp [19.0 kW] power at rated rpm 3200.

Kubota BX2660 Compact Utility Tractor featured a Kubota 1.0L 3-cyl diesel engine, 4×4 MFWD 4WD, hydrostatic power steering, 1,210 lbs [548 kg] Rear lift (at ends), 12.4 Qts [11.7 L] oil capacity and 1,389 lbs [630 kg] weight.

Kubota BX2660 Compact Utility tractor attachments include a 54″ Mower deck, 60″ Mower deck, and Kubota LA243 loader.
