Who is Zayn Malik?

The heartthrob of millions of girls and the former member of One Direction, the English boyband, Zayn Malik has accumulated millions of net worth within the decade of his presence in the music industry. 

Take Me Home, the band's platinum-selling album topped global charts. Similarly, in March 2015, he left the group and released his debut album Mind of Mine in March 2016, featuring the single "Pillowtalk." 

Zayn Malik was born 12 January 1993, in Bradford, England as Zain Javadd Malik. Zayn holds British nationality and belongs to English and Pakistani descent. 

Malik grew up in East Bowling, south of downtown Bradford and attended primary school at Lower Fields and Bradford's Tong High School. He took performing arts classes as a teenager and appeared in the productions of schools.

Malik, 17, auditioned for the seventh series of the reality-television contest The X Factor in Manchester in 2010. He sang "Let Me Love You" as his audition song by Mario's rhythm-and-blues vocalist and became the apple of an eye of the judge. 

He was eliminated before the final round of the contest, but Nicole Scherzinger and Simon Cowell the judge of the show teamed him up with fellow competitors Harry Styles, Niall Horan, Liam Payne, and Louis Tomlinson.

Performing as One Direction, in The X Factor season, this group became one of the most famous contestants. They finished the competition in the third position and instantly signed to the Syco music label from Cowell. After the signing with Syco, the rest is history. 

Not long after he left One Direction, Malik began his solo music career with the songs "I Don't Mind," "Pillowtalk" with the producer Naughty Boy which licked online. Soon there were official singles, including "Pillowtalk" and "It's You." Till date, he has released two albums, i.e. Mind of mine and Icarus Falls.
