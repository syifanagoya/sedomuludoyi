Snooki, JWoww Share Christmas Photos With Baby Girls Giovanna, Meilani

February 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Like mother, like daughter! MTV stars Nicole "Snooki" Polizzi and Jenni "JWoww" Farley spent Christmas Day together with their recently born baby girls, who are apparently already BFFs.

"Merry Christmas from us!" Snooki wrote on Instagram alongside a photo with her nearly 3-month-old daughter Giovanna Marie, BFF JWoww, and Farley's 5-month-old cutie Meilani Alexandra.

In the snap, the former Jersey Shore stars look cozy in oversized sweaters as they cradle their little ones. The same image was also posted by JWoww on Thursday, Dec. 25, with her nearly 2.5 million followers.

First-time mom JWoww also uploaded photos of Meilani and Giovanna bonding and enjoying their first-ever Christmas Days. "Seeing her BFF on Christmas," she captioned one pic of the babies. In another photo, both girls are seen clutching fingers and being pals. "Best friends holding hands," JWoww wrote.

JWoww and Snooki have been busy doting after their little ones after giving birth this past July and September, respectively.

And despite her hectic lifestyle as a mother of two, Snooki and her husband Jionni cooked a Christmas Eve feast at their home on Wednesday, Dec. 24. "Hosting Christmas Eve! We got gold chargers, a candy cane & scratch off," she joked.

Another pic showed the newlyweds preparing a meal together, which Snooki captioned: "In the kitchen getting it in with my husband."

The party didn't stop after dinner for the wild couple. After their family meal, both Snooki and Jionni went right to work on their son Lorenzo's Christmas gift. "Santa asked us to help him build Lorenzo's present," the mother of two happily wrote on Instagram. "We're elfs for the night."

Their hard work was clearly appreciated by little Lorenzo, who was seen walking around the house, "driving" his toy car. "What a happy kid," Snooki captioned the pic of her smiling son.
