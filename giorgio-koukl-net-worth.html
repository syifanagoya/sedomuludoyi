FAQs about Giorgio Koukl

Who is Giorgio Koukl?

He is a Czech-born pianist, harpsichordist, and composer based in Lugano, Switzerland. He is known for his recordings and performances of the piano music of Bohuslav Martinů and other 20th-century composers.

What is Koukl known for?

He is particularly recognized for his interpretations and recordings of the complete solo piano works of Bohuslav Martinů, as well as music by Alexander Tcherepnin, Vítězslava Kaprálová, and others.

What awards has Koukl won?

He is a prizewinner of several international music competitions, including those in Ciudad Ibaguè, Tolosa, Viotti, the H.Rahn competition, and the Aliènor Competition in Washington DC.

What labels has he recorded for?

He records exclusively for Naxos, a major classical music label.
