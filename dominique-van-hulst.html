Biography

Dominique Van Hulst was born in Valkenswaard, Netherlands on September 7, 1981. Known by the stage name Do, she is a Dutch singer most well known for providing vocals on the DJ Sammy single “Heaven.” Since her career began, she has release several successful albums including Do and Follow Me. In 2004, her hit single “”Voorbij” featuring Marco Borsato peaked at #1 on the Netherlands Top 40 chart. As a teenager, she was a talented tennis player, planning to compete in Wimbeldon until an injury at fifteen ended her tennis career. It was after her first musical performance in a cafe in Valkenswaard that she scored a recording contract.
