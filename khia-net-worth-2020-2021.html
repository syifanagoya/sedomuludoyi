Career

Khia started her professional singing career in the year 2002 when she released her debut album Thug Misses. The lead single from the album, “My Neck, My Back (Lick It),” was an instant hit and was highly appreciated by the audience. The song charted at number 42 on the Hot 100 chart and 12 on the Hot Rap tracks chart. The single was also featured on the soundtrack of Fox Broadcasting. It is also said that she wrote the lyrics of the song in 15 minutes.

In 2004, she appeared on the album of TrickDaddy “Thug Matrimony: Married to the Streets.” Khia released her second album “Gandstress” in 2006, which went upto number 67 on the Billboard R&B chart. Later, she collaborated with  Janet Jackson for her single, “So Excited,” which peaked at #90 on the Hot 100 chart.

Khia’s third album, “Nasti Muzik” was released in 2008, which also featured Atlanta rapper, Gucci Mane. In the same year, she participated in the VH1 reality show, Miss Rap Supreme. In 2010, she recorded the single “Been a Bad Girl” from her album MotorMouf aka Khia Shamone.

In 2014, she released the video song “You Deserve It” from the album “Love Locs” on YouTube. The deluxe version of the album consisted of 30 songs. Along with Ts Madison, she also hosted the web series “The Queen’s Court.”

Khia also did solo vlogging for the web series GAG ORDER. In 2018, she released her first greatest hits album, “TwerkAnomics.” It consists of her biggest longtime twerking anthems such as Steer, Santa Baby, Kash Only, Next Caller, and many more.
