Can I romance multiple characters in BG3?

July 2024 · 4 minute read

Yes, you can romance multiple characters in Baldur’s Gate 3. However, there are a few complications that you will run into while trying to get into multiple romantic relationships.

Can you romance more than one person in BG3?

Yes, you can have polyamorous relationships in Baldur’s Gate 3. However, only certain characters are open to polyamory, some are closed to it, and some will only accept it with certain characters.

How many characters can you romance in Baldur’s Gate 3?

Baldur’s Gate 3 has eight romancable characters, including all of your Origin characters and three potential companions you may meet along the way. These characters are Astarion, Gale, Halsin, Karlach, Lae’zel, Minthara, Shadowheart, and Wyll. However, there are two companions you are unable to romance, Minsc and Jaheira.

Can you have multiple characters in Baldur’s Gate 3?

Yes, you can have multiple characters in Baldur’s Gate 3. They are automatically sorted by playthrough. In early access, there were several playthroughs, both multiplayer and single, that existed at the same time. However, only the host has access to a playthrough’s save file.
