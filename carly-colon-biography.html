Carly Colon Bio (Wiki)

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Who is Carly Colon?

Carly Colon is a Puerto Rican Wrestler best known as Carlito in the WWE universe.

He is the former United States and Intercontinental Champion.

Furthermore, he is the 13-time Universal Heavyweight Champion of World Wrestling Council.

Carly Colon: Early Life Childhood and Education

Carly Colon was born on February 21, 1979, in Santurce, San Juan, Puerto Rico.

His mother is Nancy and his father, Carlos Colon Sr. is also a former Wrestler.

His two siblings,  Eddie and Stacy are also wrestlers and his second sister Melissa is not in wrestling.

He is of  Puerto Rican nationality and his ethnicity is unknown.

Carly Colon completed his graduation from Jesuit Colegio San Ignacio de Loyola.

Carly Colon: Career, Salary, and Net Worth

Carly Colon made his debut in World Wrestling Council in January 2000 and defeated Felix Tapia.

Later, he won 17 Universal Heavyweight Championships followed by a WWC Puerto Rican Heavy Weight Championship defeating Gonzalez.

Finally, in 2003 he left WWC and joined Ohio Valley Wrestling where he wrestles for one year and later signed a 7-year contract with World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE).

In October 2004, Carly Colon defeated John Cena for the United States Championship and later went on to wrestle with famous wrestlers like Shawn Michaels, Triple H, Kane, and many more.

He then won Intercontinental Championship in RAW in 2005 and became the first wrestler to win the championships 2  debut matches together.

He then formed a tag team with his brother Primo and won two consecutive Tag Team Match which includes one match at XXV WrestleMania against John Morrisson and The Miz.

In 2010 he was released from WWE for violating the code of conduct and joined the International and Independent Circuit as a promoter.

Carly Colon has an estimated net worth of $5 million as of 2022.

Carly Colon’s Rumors and Controversy

Carly Colon caused controversy when he was asked to enter a drug rehabilitation center after failing a drug test which he refused.

After his refusal, he was fired by WWE back in May 2010.

Body Measurements: Height, Weight

Carly Colon stands 5 feet and 10 inches tall and weighs around 100 kg.

His eyes and hair are in dark brown color.

Furthermore, there is no information on the measurements of his chest, biceps, and waist.
